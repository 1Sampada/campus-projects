The Process

How We
Work

A structured approach ensuring clarity, quality, and timely delivery for every project.

01

Requirement Intake

We define the project scope clearly. We discuss problem statements, technology preferences (MERN, Flutter, AI/ML), and university guidelines to gather detailed objectives and academic expectations.

02

UI/UX & System Design

Design mockups and flows are created for review. Once approved, the design is locked. Our architects plan the core logic, database structure, and ensure clean architecture patterns are followed.

03

Development

We proceed with coding using scalable, well-documented practices. Industry standards are prioritized to ensure performance, readability, and real-world applicability of the final product.

04

Delivery & Support

Upon completion, we deliver the complete package: Full Source Code, Documentation, and assistance with Live Deployment or application builds ready for presentation.

Terms and Conditions

1. Project Initiation & Payment: All projects require 50% advance payment before work begins. Work will not start without the advance payment. The remaining 50% payment must be completed before final delivery.

2. Delivery Policy: Before full payment, we will only share: UI/UX(Designs), Screenshots. Source code, live deployment, final files, and documentation will be delivered only after full payment is received.

3. UI/UX Finalization & Changes: UI/UX designs will be finalized in consultation with the client. Any reasonable changes can be made before final UI approval.

4. Urgent & Priority Requests: Projects requested on short timelines will incur additional charges. The shorter the deadline, the higher the priority fee.

5. Scope of Work: The scope of work is defined at the beginning of the project. Any features, pages, or functionalities requested outside the agreed scope will be charged separately.

6. Client Responsibility: Clients are responsible for providing: Clear requirements, Necessary content (text, references, credentials), Timely feedback. Delays from the client side may affect delivery timelines.

7. Academic Responsibility Disclaimer: Campus Projects provides educational and demonstration-based solutions. We do not guarantee academic grades, placement results, or institutional approval. Final responsibility lies with the student.

Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect: We may collect: Name, email, Phone number, College/company name, Project-related details.

2. How We Use Your Information: Your information is used only for: Project communication, Support and updates, Delivering services.

3. Data Security: All project files and personal data are handled securely. Access is limited to authorized team members only.

4. Communication: By contacting us, you agree to receive: Project-related updates, Important service communications.

Refund Policy

1. Advance Payment:The 50% advance payment is non-refundable once the project work has started. This covers planning, design, and initial development efforts.

2. Mid-Project Cancellation:If the client cancels after work has begun. No refund will be provided for completed or ongoing work.

3. Completed Projects:Once the project is completed and delivered: No refunds will be issued.

4. Exceptions:Refunds may be considered only if: Work has not started, Payment was made by mistake. Such cases are evaluated individually.
